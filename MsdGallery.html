About MSDhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, often referred to as MS Dhoni or Captain Cool, is one of India's most celebrated cricketers. Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Bihar (now Jharkhand), Dhoni is renowned for his exceptional skills as a wicketkeeper-batsman1. He has played 90 Tests, 350 ODIs, and 98 T20Is for India, leading the team to victories in the ODI World Cup, T20 World Cup, and Champions Trophy. Dhoni is also the captain of the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) in the Indian Premier League (IPL), where he continues to be a key player. Known for his calm demeanor and sharp cricketing mind, Dhoni has earned numerous accolades, including the Padma Bhushan and the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award1
